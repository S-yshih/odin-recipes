Mapo Tofu

Description

Simple dish to go with rice

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 knob of ginger (or ginger powder)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (or garlic powder)
  • ~16 oz soft tofu
  • ~0.6 oz ground pork
  • 2 scallions (optional)

For the Sauce:

  • 2.5 tbsp chili paste
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp corn starch
  • 4 tbsp water

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Combine all ingredients for the sauce in a bowl and mix well
  3. Mince garlic and cut green onion
  4. Drain the tofu and cut into approximately 1 inch cubes
  5. In a large frying pan, heat the vegetable oil on medium heat. If applicable, saute the ginger and garlic until fragrant before adding the ground pork
  6. Once the meat is cooked, add the sauce mixture and stir thoroughly
  7. Allow the sauce to boil before adding the tofu
  8. Gently mix to cover the tofu with sauce and continue to cook until heated through
  9. Add the green onions just before taking the pan off of the heat (or add it with the sauce)
  10. Serve over rice
