Pasta Bake

Description

Quick and easy, lazy meal

Ingredients

  • Jar of spaghetti sauce
  • Box of ziti noodles
  • Fresh (or shredded) mozzerella cheese
  • Cut basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Add the jar of sauce into the baking pan
  3. Fill the empty sauce jar with water and add to the baking pan
  4. Mix well and add the box of ziti noodles
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes
  6. Take the pan out of the oven and mix gently
  7. Add cheese and basil as desired
  8. Bake (uncovered) for 10 minutes or until the cheese melts
