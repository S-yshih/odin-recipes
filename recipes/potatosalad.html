Potato Salad

Description

Quick and easy, lazy cold dish

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 4 russet potatos
  • mayo
  • salt
  • sugar
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Hard boil the eggs and then cool them in cold water. Dice them when cool
  2. Dice and boil the potatos in salted water (but don't over boil)
  3. Cool down the potatos on a plate or tray
  4. Mix the potatos and eggs in a bowl, adding mayo, sugar, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Refridgerate when finished
